I Still Like Video Games

published on 23 Jun 2023

I have previously written about how video games aren’t fun any more. This is a bit of a follow up based on some recent thoughts.

Recently I have been playing some video games on Linux via Steam’s Proton and gamescope tools and was amazed that somehow this made Windows games that are notorious for instability incredibly more stable. It has been fun to play Fallout: New Vegas again (unmodded) and it just works™️!

It has been fun to just play this game again, it is one of my favourites and playing it again after so long has been a nostalgia trip I was in need of.

Which brings me to my point, I still like video games! In particular I still like the video games I used to like. The various issues of modern gaming are still prolific and getting worse but my favourite games are from before this time. I can just run them, play them and enjoy myself without having to avoid, circumvent, grind out, etc the various chores and time/money gates found in almost all modern games.

It is fun to have fun and I have found myself having fun with video games again.
